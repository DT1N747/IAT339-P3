About Me

Positive and Enthusiastic.

As an aspiring Interactive Art and Technology major at Simon Fraser University, I possess a passion for modelling and animations. I am proficient in a range of 3D modeling software, including Maya, Rhino, and Vray, I am skilled at producing high-quality 3D models with meticulous attention to detail. One of my key strengths is my ability to effectively communicate and collaborate with others. Recognizing the importance of teamwork, I actively contribute to group projects, fostering a friendly and efficient work atmosphere. By actively listening to others' perspectives, I embrace diverse ideas and incorporate them into my design process to produce innovative and inclusive solutions. With my combination of technical skills, design expertise, and collaborative mindset, I am excited to contribute to the field of UI/UX design and create meaningful experiences for users.
